Instruction: 

Press on the buttons for your choice in the game. You can also press the following keys on your keyboard for your choice.

  • 'r' - for rock
  • 'p' - for paper
  • 's' - for scissors

or

  • '1' - for rock
  • '2' - for paper
  • '3' - for scissors

Game starts when you press on any of the buttons or press any of the valid keys.

Be the first to reach 5 points to win the game.
